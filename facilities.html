FACILITIES

Classrooms

The classrooms are well lit with natural light and proper ventilation. Each classroom has CCTV surveillance to ensure safety of each child. Students are encouraged to decorate their classrooms and display their creative work on the boards.

Library

The school has a library, which is home to a number of books spread across different subjects and languages. It is also a home to plenty of novels and comic books, to encourage the reading habits among students. A media library gives access to the students to the audio-visual books and other educational documentaries.

The Art Room

Artistic pursuits produce thinking patterns and abilities that transfer to other areas of life. Be it painting or theatre, the art room is a place where a kid can explore his/her creativity beyond the books and sports.

Play Ground

Oxford English School has a campus dedicated to the playground which includes a football ground, a running trail and multi-purpose courts. It also has a play area and sand pit area for the younger kids to play and explore.

We also have an enclosed area as an outdoor shelter for various activities in case of rainy weather.

Fire Safety and Preparedness

At Oxford English School (CBSE), student safety is our top priority The campus is equipped with modern fire extinguishers strategically placed at key locations to ensure quick access during emergencies.

Computer Lab

The school has a well-equipped computer lab with modern computers and internet access. Students learn computer skills and use educational software as part of their regular classes. The lab helps students stay updated with technology in a fun and safe environment.

Science Lab

Oxford English School (CBSE) has a well-equipped science lab where students can do hands-on experiments. The lab has all the tools and materials needed for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology activities. It helps students understand science better through practical learning.

Maths Lab

A well equipped Maths Lab where students can explore math concepts through fun activities and models.

Kreedo Lab

Kreedo Lab for young learners providing fun, hands-on learning materials that help children develop basic skills through play.

Kids Play Area

Safe and fun indoor play area for kids with a soft green carpet and fully meshed for protection.

Smart Board

Smart boards are set up in each of the classrooms to make learning more interactive and interesting.

Robotic Lab Facilities

Our school is proud to offer a fully equipped Robotic Lab designed to inspire innovation and hands-on learning in the field of robotics and automation.
