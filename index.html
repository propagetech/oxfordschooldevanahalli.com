ABOUT US

Who We Are!!

Oxford English School is a co-educational school, located at Shanthinagar, Devanahalli, Bangalore Rural District Spread over 8,600 Sq.ft under Vijiayabharathi Education Society®, #129, VSSPC Complex, Govindappa Road, Basavanagudi, Bengaluru. Our intention is to enable a progressive, child-centric and holistic development of students.

OES is committed towards imparting quality holistic education by giving students the opportunities to develop their creative and social skills through a wide variety of programmes in a safe, innovative and healthy environment. We invoke in students a love of learning through the development of the intellectual, emotional, social, physical and creative potentials.

PROGRAMMES

SCHOLASTIC PROGRAMMES

We regard Teaching students how to learn is just as important as teaching them what to learn. At OES, we encourage our students to think for themselves, ask questions and pursue their interests through independent reading and research. All this is ensured through unique learning pedagogy with critical inputs of our advisory board of management who are lifelong teachers and learners.

Early Years Programme

Kindergarten is offered at the early childhood level. Children join Kindergarten at three years of age and continue for three years until they graduate to Grade 1.

Middle and Secondary Years Programme

CO-SCHOLASTIC PROGRAMMES

Lifelong learning evolves when a holistic eco-system is nurtured in its true spirit. OES strives to offer array of opportunities to channelize the innate talents of every child beyond the classroom to realize their multiple-intelligences and potential. Visual Arts, Music, Drama, Yoga and Physical Education are additional curricular offerings to all the students.

Visual and Performing Arts

Arts education, broadly categorized as visual and performing arts includes music, dance, theatre and creative art, plays a significant role in the all-round development of the student.

Physical Education

The union of a healthy mind within a healthy body emerges from the well-structured programme which integrates Physical Education in the curriculum.

The School Clubs

Students are offered options of participating in various clubs which provide ample scope for identifying talents and honing multi skills while interacting with their peer and mentors.
