ADMISSIONS

Registrations shall be invited through communication on website/ other medias. Once the registration process for an academic year is open, parents would be able to access the registration portal through link as well can avail registration forms from the office.

The process of registration is as follows:

Online Registrations:

Parents seeking admission for their child to Oxford English School are requested to fill the registration form using the link provided in the admission communication.. Once the admission is confirmed, you will be automatically redirected to the payment gateway where you have to make the payment for the registration form. Incomplete or unpaid registrations will not be accepted for admissions. After registrations Proficiency assessment in terms of entrance exams are held to assess the previous academic knowledge to aid in the process of admission

Steps to follow to apply (once in the registration portal) :
  • Fill registration form online for enquiry.
  • Visit Office and Know more about the school and if you are happy Pay fee on Office Desk.
Click Here Fill The Form | Register Online

Registrations at Office Desk

Parents seeking admissions for their child to Oxford English School are requested to collect the registration form at the Office. Once the registration is completed Proficiency assessment in terms of entrance exams will be conducted to assess the previous academic knowledge to aid in the process of admission. Parents are required to complete the admission process with the payment of I term fee.


PROFICIENCY ASSESSMENT
Students are required to take a proficiency assessment which will be based on the syllabus of the previous academic year.

For Nursery and KG 1, there will be no Proficiency Assessment.
