CO-SCHOLASTIC PROGRAMMES

Lifelong learning evolves when a holistic eco-system is nurtured in its true spirit. OES strives to offer array of opportunities to channelize the innate talents of every child beyond the classroom to realize their multiple-intelligences and potential. Visual Arts, Music, Drama, Yoga and Physical Education are additional curricular offerings to all the students. There are also many enrichment activities including field trips, exhibitions, social gatherings, EAB etc.

Visual and Performing Arts

Arts education, broadly categorized as visual and performing arts includes music, dance, theatre and creative art, plays a significant role in the all-round development of the student. The development of creative ability enhances the neural systems that benefit motor skills, originality and emotional balance.

Physical Education

The union of a healthy mind within a healthy body emerges from the well-structured programme which integrates Physical Education in the curriculum. Apart from building motor skills and coordination, it significantly impacts the development of the brain and therefore the behavior. There is a provision of outdoor and indoor sports activities such as Football, Volleyball, Badminton, Cricket, Yoga, Aerobics, Athletics, Throw ball and Chess along with other indoor games. Inter-House and Inter-school competitions impart life lessons such as discipline, team spirit, peer relationship, patience, cooperation, stress management and leadership qualities.

The School Clubs

Students are offered options of participating in various clubs which provide ample scope for identifying talents and honing multi skills while interacting with their peer and mentors. The clubs include Environment, Health and Wellness, Math, Science and Quiz. The clubs foster activities, debates, awareness campaigns, community service as well as steps to combat environmental and social issues.

Cultural Programs

Our school takes pride in nurturing the artistic and cultural talents of our students through a variety of vibrant cultural programs held throughout the year. These events provide a platform for students to showcase their creativity, confidence, and appreciation for diverse traditions. From music and dance performances to drama, art exhibitions, and festival celebrations, our cultural programs promote teamwork, self-expression, and a sense of community. By participating in these enriching activities, students develop a deeper understanding of cultural heritage and grow into well-rounded individuals.

Sports & Co-curricular Activities

We believe in holistic education that goes beyond the classroom. Our school offers a wide range of sports and co-curricular activities that help students explore their interests, develop life skills, and grow in confidence. From athletics and team sports to music, art, drama, and club activities, every student is encouraged to participate and shine.

House Activities

Our school’s vibrant house system—Rose, Daffodils, Orchids, and Dianthus—encourages a spirit of unity, leadership, and friendly competition. Students actively participate in a range of inter-house events including sports, debates, quizzes, and cultural programs. These activities promote teamwork, creativity, and holistic development, while fostering a strong sense of belonging and school pride.

Observing Important Days & Events

Our school actively observes all important national and international days, festivals, and awareness events to instill values, cultural understanding, and social responsibility among students. Through special assemblies, activities, and themed programs, we encourage students to reflect on the significance of each occasion and become more informed, empathetic global citizens.

Graduation Day

Graduation Day is a proud and emotional milestone in our school’s calendar. For our Kindergarten students, it's a joyful celebration marking the completion of their early learning journey, filled with songs, performances, and smiles. For our SSLC students, it’s a moment of reflection and achievement as they step forward into new academic and life adventures. Both events honor the growth, efforts, and accomplishments of our students, while celebrating the support of parents and teachers who guide them along the way.

OES Fest – Halli Sogadu | Makkala Santhe

This year’s OES Fest was a vibrant celebration of our rich rural heritage with the nostalgic theme “Halli Sogadu”, meaning “Essence of Village Life”.
Makkala santhe is a fun-filled, hands-on learning experience designed to spark creativity and entrepreneurial spirit in our students.

Annual Day Celebration – OES

The Annual Day at OES is a grand celebration of talent, achievements, and school spirit. It is a much-awaited event that brings together students, parents, and staff in an evening filled with vibrant performances, inspiring speeches, and recognition of excellence.

Public Speaking & Communication Skills

At Oxford English School, we actively nurture confident communicators. Through debates, elocutions, assemblies, storytelling sessions, and stage performances, students are encouraged to express themselves effectively, build self-confidence, and develop strong public speaking skills—essential for leadership and future success.
