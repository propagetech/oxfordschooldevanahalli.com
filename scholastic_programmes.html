SCHOLASTIC PROGRAMMES

We regard Teaching students how to learn is just as important as teaching them what to learn. At OES, we encourage our students to think for themselves, ask questions and pursue their interests through independent reading and research. All this is ensured through unique learning pedagogy with critical inputs of our advisory board of management who are lifelong teachers and learners.

Our curriculum is updated regularly to enable the students to efficiently adapt to improvised methods of learning. Our teaching methodologies are also reviewed to ensure that teaching stimulates students’ interest. We are provided with advanced teaching aids that assist the teachers to make the classes as interactive as possible and use different methods to get the best out of each student.

Collaborative learning is incorporated into lessons so that students can share ideas and learn with and from each other with peer interest. Students are engaged in experiential learning through a variety of resources. There are also many enrichment activities including field trips, interclass activities, Science exhibitions, workshops etc.

It is our constant endeavor to facilitate our students to learn to be collaborators, innovators, and creative thinkers who will be ready to join the stream and emerge as Global citizens.

Early Years Programme

Kindergarten is offered at the early childhood level. Children join Kindergarten at three years of age and continue for three years until they graduate to Grade 1.

Primary Years Programme

OES offers an integrated, interdisciplinary curriculum for primary school. CBSE curriculum is under integration with experiential learning resources to engage the students physically, cognitively and emotionally. We emphasize on developing strong academic base for the students along with the opportunities to develop their creative and social skills through a wide variety of programmes.

Middle and Secondary Years Programme

Middle School students study a curriculum that is broad based with emphasis on the core subjects – Mathematics, Science, Social Science and Languages. Students are engaged in experiential learning through a variety of resources. The focus continues to ensure that our students excel in academics and with overall holistic development along with Art, Music, Drama and Physical Education.
