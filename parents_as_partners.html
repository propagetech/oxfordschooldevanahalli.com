PARENTS AS PARTNERS

OES believes that parents’ involvement in education is very important in the learning process. The active involvement of the parents in children’s education will aid in betterment. Beyond classroom also, the encouragement and support of parents is found to be essential for participation in various kinds of activities and overall development of a child. As a school, we believe in a partnership between the parents and school to support the learning needs of a child, which will eventually lead to better outcomes in a harmony. To promote a healthy relationship between the parents and teachers, we have regular Parent teachers’ meets to discuss academic, personal and social developments of their child.
